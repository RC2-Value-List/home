Demand Terminology

High Demand High Demand is given to items that receive more than 7 vouches in a week.
Medium Demand Medium Demand status is given to items that receive more than 4 vouches but less than 7 vouches in a week.
Low Demand Low Demand status is given to items that receive more than 1 vouch but less than 4 vouches in a week.
Extremely Low Demand Extremely Low Demand status is given to items that receive less than 1 vouch in 2 weeks.
Unknown Demand Unknown Demand status is given to items that do not have enough vouches, and/or is difficult to determine a demand via market activity.

Value Terminology

Obtainable Obtainable is a status that is given to items that arent bought from an ingame NPC shop and hold value.
Limited Limited is a status given to items that were previously obtainable via legitemate means but are no longer obtainable. Limited time event items are a great example of this.

Miscellaneous

Loose (Loose) is usually added to the end of an item's name if it has 2 states to distinguish values between them. Loose Items are also commonly referred to as "Unboxed" items within the community.
Boxed (Boxed) is usually added to the end of an item's name if it has 2 states to distinguish values between them. Items in this state are contained within a box. Some Items only have a boxed version (for example, the Snowbreaker Spawner, due to the "loose" version being a structure).
Unknown Value Similar to Unknown Demand, Unknown Value is a status given to items that don't have enough vouches, and/or is difficult to determine a value via market activity.
